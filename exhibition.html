வணக்கம்

WELCOME TO OUR
VIRTUAL EXHIBITION

It is a collective and community-driven project, organised by volunteers attached to the Tamil Information Centre. This exhibition is a project born from loss; we work in the wake of the loss and suffering of Mullivaikal, determined to remember the suffering of the Tamil people. We also mourn the passing of TIC's executive director V.S. Nadarajah. His vision and guidance this work has been a challenge, but we hope that we have been true to his vision, and have chosen to mark 18 May known as Tamil Genocide Day amongst many Tamil's by hosting this exhibition that demonstrates the resilience and creativity of the community despite the violence inflicted upon them.
